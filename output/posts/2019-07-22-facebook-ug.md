# Facebook ug

Facebook ug Monday, May 21, 2018 6:45 AM Facebook has all your friends and associates, but not much tooling to collaborate with them. Everyone can project into the feed but there is little to no shared creation and most interaction is superficial. The "look at me, look at me" that FB is now instead needs to become let us --together -- do something meaningful. FB has APIs for independent developers; but such APIs don't allow those developers to add real substance. Search is lame; and semantic relationships - how add? I would like tools to collaborate with my FB friends in shared interest groups. FB has groups. and Events. but needs joint writing and organization tools. Collaboration tools.
